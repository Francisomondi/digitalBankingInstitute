Welcome to Digital Banking & Finance Proffessional Institute

Digital Banking & Finance Institution was founded in 2014 as a pure Banking institution teaching student fundamentals of banking and Finance,Digital Banking nurtured, packaged and worked with several students in various capacities, thereby producing some of the biggest and well known bankers across Kenya. Since its Foundation, The institution has made numerous steps making sure all sectors of our economiy is well touched with the introduction of a number of courses ranging from ICT and Engineering to Hospitality Management. The school is currently Offering over 15 courses including Mass communication and media studies. With our intakes in January, May and September we encourage the general public to enroll in our courses available Here

STUDENT ACCOMODATION

Accomodation is offered to our students at hostels , which are equiped with modern facilities e.g hot water , clean spacious rooms with lockers , 24 hour security , colour Tvs Breakfast and Supper are provided from Monday to Friday. Breakfast Lunch and Supper provided on all Weekends and Public Holidays. There is also a support system to Students adapting to College life.

2. VISION

To be the champion in skillful, innovative and technical training in Africa.

3. MISSION STATEMENT

To develop self-reliant, highly empowered and competitively employable trainees for a progressive nation.

4. GRADES REQUIRED FOR KNEC EXAMINED COURSES.

  • Diploma ; C-(Minus) and Above
  • Certificate; D Plain and Above
  • Artisan level ; D-(Minus) and below including KCPE and Secondary School Drop-out
  • ECDE Certificate ; D+ Plus and Above
  • ECDE Diploma ; C Plain and Above
  • ECDE Proficiency ; D Plain and Below

5. OTHER ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS

  • Result Slip & Birth Certificate.
  • Copies of National ID Card, KCSE Certificate or
  • Three Recent coloured Passport Size Photos.
  • Personal effects for students wishing to reside in Hostels.
  • Continous Assesment Test (CAT) fee Kshs.1000/= Per Term
  • External Examination Fee; to be notified during admission
  • Practical Fee; to be notified during admission
  • Graduation Fee Ksh 2000/=(Paid once during the last term of the Course)
  • Caution Fee (Refundable after graduation) Kshs 1000/=
  • Activity Fee Kshs. 1000/= per term
  • Admission fee of Kshs. 5,000/= (Payable Once).
  • Student Course handbook, exercise book, student I/D & college T-Shirt of Kshs. 4,500/= (payable once)
  • Attachment Fee Kshs. 2800/= ( Paid once during the last term of the entire course)
  • Business Plan Fee ( KNEC & ABMA students only) Kshs. 1000/=
  • Teaching Practice Assessment ( ECDE students only) Kshs. 5000/=
